Why joining?

Joining the IAML is completely free: simply fill the form below, and wait for the confirmation email. By joining our association, you will receive our newsletter with invitations to all our events, a curated selection of news and initiatives around Italy, exclusive member discounts, and much more!

Due to a large backlog of requests and an upcoming reorganization, registrations are currently closed. Follow us on our social media pages to stay updated on the news!
